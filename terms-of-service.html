⭑ TERMS OF SERVICE ⭑

- By commissioning me, you accept this terms of service -

Payments & Refunds

  • I accept payment via Paypal invoicing ONLY
  • Payment must be made in full within 72 hours unless a payment plan was agreed upon between both parties.
  • Commissioners may receive a full refund if:
    • The artist cancels the commission.
    • The artist does not complete the commission within the due date.
    • The commissioner cancels the before any work has begun.
  • If the commissioner wishes to cancel after I have begun working, they will receive a refund approximate to the work completed so far.
  • No refunds will given for completed artwork.

Commission process

  • I reserve the right to decline a commission for any reason
  • If I have accepted your commission, I will let you know via your preferred contact method and send an invoice to your Paypal.
  • Once payment has been made, you have secured your spot in the queue.
  • I will provide WIPs for every major step of the process (sketch, lines, flats etc.) however the commissioner is welcome to ask for updates at anytime.
  • Edits Policy:
    • The commissioner is expected to inform the artist of any edits they want at any given stage of the process.
    • If the commissioner has given the OK to move forward with the artwork, they will no longer be be able to request edits to the previous WIP(s).
    • Stylistic edits will not be permitted.
    • Edits to the completed artwork are not permitted unless it is the fault of the artist.
  • Upon completion, the commissioner will receive both the high resolution and smaller, watermarked version of the artwork.
  • The commissioner is not permitted to share the high resolution copy as it is intended for personal use only.
  • The commissioner is welcome to post/share the smaller, watermarked copy as they please. Credit is not required but is very much appreciated.

Deadlines

  • By default, the deadline for commissions is 2 weeks after payment has been made.
  • The commissioner must let me know upfront if they need the commission by a certain date.
  • There will be a $50 rush fee if the commission is needed in 3 days or less.
  • I will only accept rush commissions if I feel confident in completing them without sacrificing quality of the artwork.

Copyright & Usage policy

  • All characters depicted in commmissioned artwork belong to their respective owners. However the artist retains copyright to the artwork itself
  • Commissions are intended for personal use only.
  • Redistribution of the artwork for use in printed merchandise or as promotion goods, services or social media pages is prohibited.
  • Commissioners do not have the right to mint/sell their commission for NFT
  • If the commissioner is found to be violating these terms, they will be blacklisted from commissioning me in the future.
