TERMS OF SERVICE

- By commissioning me, you accept this Terms of Service -

  1. Payments & Refunds

    • I accept payments via Paypal ONLY
    • Payment must be made in full within 72 hours unless otherwise discussed.
    • Clients may receive a full refund if:
      • The artist cancels the commission
      • The artist does not complete the commission within due time
      • The client cancels the commission before any work has begun
    • If the client wishes to cancel after i have begun working, they will receive a partial refund approximate to the work completed so far
    • No refunds will be given for completed artwork

  2. Edits Policy

    • The client will receive updates for each stage of the commission (sketch, lines, flat colors etc.)
    • Large edits must be made during the sketch stage of a commission. Smaller edits may be made at any other stage.
    • Mistakes at the fault of the artist will be corrected at any stage.
    • If a mistake was made because the reference image was unlcear, outdated, or the relevant info was not provided, the artist is not at fault and requesting to correct it may come with a fee
    • Minor adjustments may be made to finished artwork, but there will be a limit to such edits before a fee may be required

  3. Deadlines

    • Commissions will be completed within 1 month of payment
    • If the client requires a deadline of 5 days or less, there will be a $50 rush fee

  4. Rights

    • All commissioned artwork is intended for personal use only.
    • Redistribution of my artwork for use in printed merchandise or as promotion of goods, services or social media pages is prohibited.
    • All characters depicted within my artwork belong to their respective owners. However, the artist retains copyright to the image itself.
    • The client will receive a smaller, watermarked version of commissioned artwork that they may share online.
    • Clients DO NOT have the right to mint/sell their commission for NFT.
