Terms of Service

Last updated 7/13/2024

1. Payments & Refunds

  • All payments will be made via Paypal invoicing only.
  • Clients are not permitted to send unsolicited payment and will be immediately refunded if done so.
  • Full refunds are only possible if I have yet to begin working on the artwork. Otherwise the amount is depending on work completed thus far* (see section 3)
  • I reserve the right to cancel and refund a commission at any time.

2. Commission Process

  • You will receive a rough, colored sketch at the start of each commission. At this stage you may request major edits.
  • Once the sketch is approved, I will begin working on the remainder of the commission.
  • Before finishing touches are made, you will receive one final WIP where you may request minor edits.
  • After final approval, any more edits will come with a fee. The only exception are mistakes made on my end, which I will fix at no addtional cost.
  • Once completed, you will receive a high resolution copy of the completed artwork. You may also request a low resolution copy if you plan to share the art.

3. Timeframes

  • Every commission is due to be completed within 90 days of payment. After which you are entitled to receive a full refuned regardless of how far in the process I am.
  • The actual completion time is unique to each commission based on many factors (complexity, postion in queue, LIFE etc.)
  • While I do my best to give a as accurate an ETA as possible, please understand that (unlike the 90 days) this is not a concrete deadline. There may be things outside of my control that may impact completion time.

4. Copyright & Usage

  • Clients are permitted to use commissioned work for non-commercial purposes only.
  • Copyright for the artwork remains with the artist. Characters belong to their respective owners.
  • I reserve the right to publish all of my artwork.
