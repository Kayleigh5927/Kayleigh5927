Rebranding

October 12th, 2024

After a ton of thought, i am officially rebranding to the name Scarlet Eclipse! I will miss the name Scarlet Spectrum, i've had it forever! But, i feel that it is tied to the My Little Pony fandom. Don't get me wrong, i still love the show! However, over the years i have expanded my art to other things. For a long time i have wanted to rebrand in order to reflect that change, but i'd been too afraid to risk it. I just decided to rip the bandaid off today. I know you guys will miss the old name, but i'm still here! Same art new name, just entering a new chapter! :D
