Terms of Service

- By commissioning me, you accept this terms of service -

Payments & Refunds

  • I accept payment via Paypal invoicing ONLY
  • Payment must be made in full within 72 hours unless otherwise discussed.
  • Clients may receive a full refund if:
    • The artist cancels the commission.
    • The artist does not complete the commission within the due date.
    • The commissioner cancels the before any work has begun.
  • If the commissioner wishes to cancel after i have begiun working, they will receive a refund approximate to the work completed so far.
  • No refunds will given for completed artwork.

Commission process

  • I reserve the right to decline a commission for any reason
  • If i have accepted your commission, i will let you know via your preferred contact method and you will be added to the queue. I will be sending an invoice out only when I am ready to begin your commission.
  • Full payment must me made before I begin any work, unless a payment plan was agreed upon between both parties.
  • I will provide WIPs for every major step of the process (sketch, lines, flats etc.) however the commissioner is welcome to ask for updates at anytime.
  • Edits Policy:
    • If the commissioner is expected to inform the artist of any edits they want at any given stage of the process. e.g. I will not make edits to the lineart after it has already been approved and I moved on
    • Stylistic edits will not be permitted.
    • Edits to the completed artwork are not permitted unless it is the fault of the artist.
  • Upon completion, the commissioner will receive a high resolution copy of the artwork. They are not permitted to share the high resolution copy as it is intended for personal use only.
  • If the commissioner wishes to share the artwork, they are welcome request the watermarked version at anytime.

Deadlines

  • By default, the deadline for commissions is 2 weeks after payment has been made.
  • The commissioner must let me know upfront if they need the commission by a certain date.
  • There will be a $50 rush fee if the commission is needed in 3 days or less.
  • I will only accept rush commissions if I feel confident in completing them without sacrificing quality of the artwork.
